Staff

The all-volunteer staff of Operation Code coordinates, integates and synchronizes efforts to accomplish the mission. Say hello.

Rick Rein
Director, Software Mentor Protégé Program

Rick is a ruby/rails/elixer software engineer at Akamai Technologies, is on the core team, a Turing mentor, and a Marine veteran. Rick blogs at http://rickre.in and can be reached at rick@operationcode.org.

Jeremy Hall (@lordfauntleroy1)
Technical Product Manager

Jeremy Hall is our Technical Product Manager and helps on our flagship site, operationcode.org, a software and Army veteran. Jeremy can be reached at jeremy@operationcode.org.

Nell Shamrell-Harrington (@nellshamrell)
Chair, Scholarship Program

Nell Shamrell is a software engineer at Chef, daughter of two U.S. Air Force officers, and runs the Operation Code Scholarship Program. Nell blogs at http://nellshamrell.com and can be reached at nell@operationcode.org.

Doug Brown
Scholarship Outreach

Doug Brown is a software engineer at Apple, a new dad, and our Scholarship Outreach. Doug can be reached at doug@operationcode.org.

Maggi Molina
Director, Employer Services & Fellowship Program

Maggi Molina is an U.S. Air Force veteran, former Co-Founder & Chief Operating Officer at VetStartups, and runs our Employer Services. Maggi can be reached at maggi@operationcode.org.

George Everts
Code School Outreach Specialist

George Everts is a 27-year U.S. Army retired Noncommissioned Officer, a veteran of Operation Desert Storm, and is our liaison to code schools nationally. George can be reached at george@operationcode.org.

Krystyna Ewing (@wimsy113)
Deploy Developer & Community Evangelist

Krysytna Ewing is an Iraq-war Army veteran, former student of Dev Bootcamp, Zinc web apprentice, and a Deploy developer. Krystyna can be reached at krystyna@operationcode.org.

Nathanael Duke
Re-Deploy Veterans Transition Program

Nathanael Duke is an Army veteran and support engineer at OCLC, software developer, and Program Manager of Re-Deploy Veterans Transition Program. Nathanael can be reached at nathanael@operationcode.org.

Rob Kriner
Marketing & Communications

Rob Kriner is an UI/UX Product Manager at Corsearch, IAVA mentor, and Communications & Marketing Advisor to Operation Code. Rob can be reached at rob@operationcode.org.

Farrah Morrisey
Director of Development

Farrah Morrisey is a 12-year fund development veteran and grantwriter with regional and national experience, and our Director of Development. Farrah can be reached at farrah@operationcode.org.

Bradley Cantor
Head of Public Policy

Bradley Cantor is a former legislative director for Oregon Senator Mark Hass, former legislative aide to Congressman Blumenauer on the hill, and leads our public policy efforts. Bradley can be reached at bradley@operationcode.org.

Conrad Hollomon (@hollomancer)
Chief of Staff/Operations

Conrad Hollomon is an Army Captain, National Guardsmen, test engineer at SparkMeter. Conrad can be reached at conrad@operationcode.org.

David Molina (@davidcmolina)
Founder & Executive Director
